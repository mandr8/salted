Welcome to the Guardian's User Research Panel
You are receiving this email because you have a Guardian account and we would like to get your help improving our digital products.

  • Get an insider's view of our product development
  • Help the Guardian's development teams create products that are truly meaningful for our readers
  • Receive a monthly newsletter with a summary of our latest research and how you've helped us
